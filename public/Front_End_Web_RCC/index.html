About

The company Research Collaborations Community (RCC) was established on May 25, 2019. Based at Jl. Riung Ampuh No.3, Riung Bandung, Bandung City 40295, West Java, Indonesia. The association aims to gather individuals who share a common interest in research and scientific collaboration. Since its establishment, the Research Collaboration Community Association has endeavored to become a forum for researchers and academics to share knowledge, resources, and innovative ideas to encourage the advancement of science and technology in Indonesia.
